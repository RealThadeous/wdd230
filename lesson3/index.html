SITE PURPOSE:

Have you ever looked at the weather before taking that hour long trip just to get there to discover the information you received was wrong? Do you want to know what there is to do when the weather changes?

Weather Friends is a site where people may interact with one another by asking questions related to weather in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven so that you don't have any more surprises when you are on that day trip with your family.

Friends (or Community Members) may also post suggestions for activities so that you can be prepared incase you need a Plan B. Our site is all about helping one another to avoid a ruined trip, by giving actual facts of the weather in certain areas, and at the same time providing additional activity ideas.

And yes...we also provide weather reports.
