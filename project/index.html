THE GEORGE BUSINESS CHAMBER

George Business Chamber advocates for the advancement of business and community. We are a non-profit, membership-based organization that collaborates with the other Business Chambers/ Organisations in the Garden Route to ensure economic growth, development, and prosperity. We conduct our business and interactions with stakeholders in an ethical manner and take a strong stand against non-ethical behaviour. The George Business Chamber NPC Southern Cape is lead and governed by a Board, Executive Committee (Exco)and 2 full-time employees of which one is the CEO. Our portfolio focus is determined by the socio-economic environment. Each portfolio is lead by a Board or Exco member and supported by appropriately skilled affiliates.
